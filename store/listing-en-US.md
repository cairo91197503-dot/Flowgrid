# Long description
Welcome to FlowGrid, a relaxing yet challenging logic puzzle inspired by zen stone gardens. Your mission is simple: rotate the pipe pieces to connect the water source to the destination, finding harmony on the board.

WHAT YOU'LL FIND:
• Daily Level: Every day brings a new challenge that is identical for players worldwide. Compare your results and maintain your streak!
• Endless Mode: Play non-stop with procedurally generated levels. The difficulty (and board size) increases gradually.
• Stress-free: Play at your own pace, with the soothing soundtrack and haptic feedback creating a zen experience.
• Accessibility: Built-in colorblind mode ensures everyone can visualize the water and energy flows perfectly.
